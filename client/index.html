How to Play

Controls

  • W / Up Arrow - Accelerate
  • S / Down Arrow - Brake / Slow Down
  • A / D / Left / Right - Steer
  • Mouse - Aim direction
  • Left Click - Fire weapon
  • M - Place mine (when equipped)
  • Space - Drift

Weapons

Collect weapon crates scattered around the map. Weapon types:

AUTO-AIM Machine Gun, SMG, Shotgun, Laser, Dual Gun
MANUAL-AIM Rocket, Bomb, Grenade, Cannon, Mine

Objective

Destroy enemy cars to score kills. Win by having the most kills when time runs out, or reach the kill limit first!
